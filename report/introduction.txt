4. Introduction\nAgriculture is vital for food security, yet smallholder farmers in Africa face persistent crop health challenges. With 50% smartphone penetration in Kenya (GSMA 2024), web-based solutions like AgriGuide leverage browser accessibility to deliver AI diagnostics, expert advice, and market linkages. This report details AgriGuide's features, architecture, and alignment with global agricultural innovations, supported by code in lib/actions.ts and lib/data.ts.
